About Rhett’s Tree Service

Family-Owned Values & Know-How

Rhett’s Tree Service is a family-run outfit based right here in Northwest Arkansas. We specialize in tree removal, trimming, canopy shaping, storm clean-up and irrigation services—bringing thoughtful care to every corner of your outdoor space. We do it the way it ought to be done: with grit, care, and a handshake you can trust. Around here, we show up on time, work hard, and leave your yard with respect—we clean up right, keep things safe, and finish the job like we’d want it done at our own place.

This business grew out of a simple idea: folks deserve honest work from people who know what they’re doing. Rhett has years of hands-on experience with chainsaws, landscaping, and the kind of problem-solving that only comes from tough jobs in real yards. Whether it’s clearing a downed oak after a storm or shaping up a backyard for summer shade, he approaches each job with skill, care, and a commitment to doing things right.
